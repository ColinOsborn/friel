About Us

Design Collaboration - FRIEL Construction can bring an important perspective to the design review process from master planning to detail drawing review.

Team Building - If an architect, designer or decorator is not on board, we can guide you through the selection process.

System Planning and Design - Is a mechanical engineer or lighting designer needed?  We guide you through the pros and cons of these decisions and help build the team of professional consultants.

Detailed Estimating and Value Engineering - Estimating is not a one time event. As the plans and ideas evolve from concept design to construction documents, so do our estimates. Beginning with early pre-construction planning, we continually analyze the information at every phase of the project and provide creative ideas for cost and time savings.

Dan Friel

Owner and President

An Aspen resident since 1985, Dan began his building career nearly 35 years ago. After four years of construction technical training in Philadelphia and Lancaster, Pennsylvania he founded Friel Construction and began focusing on custom home construction and remodeling. Dan’s lifelong philosophy is to obtain the best product for the best price, without compromising quality. Dan maintains daily involvement in construction projects and is routinely present on each job site. Dan’s work can be seen in photo’s on this web site and others upon request.

General Contracting

We employ a team of carpenters and technicians, available for home care and maintenance services — from moderate servicing to full remodel. Our range of work includes construction of the highest quality homes, commercial tenant finish-outs, contemporary remodels, restoration of historic structures, and the design and construction of equestrian estates. Friel Construction has extensive experience in a wide range of architectural styles and finishes, from classic traditional to mountain rustic to ultra-contemporary.

Remodeling

Friel Construction has extensive experience in the remodeling business from small scale to expansive additions. We specialize in fast track construction, providing your architect’s vision with a responsible and accurate timeline. Our efficient scheduling is focused on reducing costs and waste.

Green Building

We focus on using building resources in a sustainable manner, providing healthy indoor environments and maximizing energy efficiency as a green building company. We also offer a team of consultants and tradesmen to guide you through the options and associated benefits environmentally conscious building provides.

Communication

Proactive management is based on clear and concise communication with all parties of the building team.

Before Construction - Friel Construction completes a full review of risks, unknown costs, and assumptions. Once construction begins, close monitoring of the project is provided through biweekly construction updates and budget reviews.

Precise and Timely Billing - We employ a full time, professional office staff charged with payroll processing, monthly billing, tracking and auditing subcontracts, as well as managing insurances, liability and lien wavers. We incorporate fully integrated budgeting, scheduling and accounting software systems. Friel Construction has a proven system of checks and balances to ensure all administration is detailed and accurate.
